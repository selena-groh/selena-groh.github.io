Selena Groh | Biography

Selena Groh is a sophomore at Tufts University double-majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive and Brain Sciences. She was born and raised near Chicago, IL, and she is an alumna of Buffalo Grove High School. Her interests include web design, problem solving, and the intersection of cognition and computers. She has extensive experience in theatre, particularly in set construction, stage management, and producing. She spends her free time knitting and drinking hot chocolate no matter the season.
